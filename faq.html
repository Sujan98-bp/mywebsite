Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I book a service? +
You can book a service through our website or mobile app. Simply select the service you want, choose your preferred date and time, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is confirmed.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and online banking. All payments are processed securely through our platform.

Massage Services

What should I prepare before a massage session? +
We recommend taking a shower before the session, wearing comfortable clothing, and ensuring you have a quiet, clean space for the massage. Our therapist will bring all necessary equipment.
Are your massage therapists certified? +
Yes, all our massage therapists are certified professionals with proper training and experience. They undergo regular training and quality checks to maintain our high service standards.

Beauty Services

What beauty services do you offer? +
We offer a wide range of beauty services including facials, manicures, pedicures, hair care, and makeup services. Check our beauty services page for a complete list of offerings.
What products do you use for beauty treatments? +
We use high-quality, branded products that are safe and suitable for different skin types. Our beauty professionals can recommend specific products based on your skin type and concerns.

Home Cleaning

What cleaning supplies do I need to provide? +
Our cleaning professionals bring their own cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer specific products to be used in your home, please let us know in advance.
How long does a typical cleaning service take? +
The duration depends on the size of your home and the type of cleaning service you've booked. A standard cleaning for a 2-bedroom apartment typically takes 2-3 hours.
